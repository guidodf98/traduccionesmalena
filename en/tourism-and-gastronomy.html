Tourism and Gastronomy

From my specializations, this is the one I enjoy the most! Why? Because it merges my two true passions: travelling and translating.

If your business belongs to the touristic, hospitality or gastronomic industry, customer satisfaction is essential; you should make your visitors “feel at home”. To achieve this, communication is key: your texts should be clear, original, catchy and they should be translated.

Too much information? Relax! I can write, proofread and translate your texts.

I’ll help you with:

  • Your travel guides and maps
  • Your leaflets and ads
  • Your business website, your blog or social networks
  • The subtitles for your videos, vlogs and podcasts
  • Your travel articles and reviews
  • And more!
